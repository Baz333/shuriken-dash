Shuriken Dash

A Unity-based first-person rhythm shooter video game with procedurally generated audio

What is Shuriken Dash about?

In Shuriken Dash, players take control of Kyo, as they use their special shuriken to elimate a group of raiders trying to steal it. When the player kills an enemy with the shuriken, they teleport to them and throw the shuriken again, killing every enemy in the level. Watch out though! If the player misses, the shuriken will break and the player has to restart the level. Memorise the enemy's locations, and figure out a path that hits each raider in the quickest time possible!
